# Food Sonificator
Using Spotify api's attributes of songs, and arduino and processing

in order to play a song when you put your ear near your food.
When food is getting older, your songs are getting sadder.

youtube link - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FoRTQwM_alM&ab_channel=YuvalEzrati
